About Me

I’m Zeqian Li, a designer and creative technologist exploring how interactive media, UX design, and emerging technologies can shape more human-centered futures.

I’m currently pursuing my graduate studies in Information Experience Design (IXD) at Pratt Institute, a program that encourages the integration of design, research, and technology to craft meaningful experiences.

My approach bridges art and innovation — rooted in my background in Photography & Video from the School of Visual Arts and expanded through speculative design, interactive installations, and digital products. At Pratt, I continue developing work that invites reflection, empathy, and participation through thoughtful interaction.

This website showcases selected design works, research projects, and collaborations that reflect my journey at the intersection of creativity, technology, and social impact.
